## What drives the price

Rates usually reflect the coach's experience, the format (in-person vs. online),
and session length. A coach who is also a working, award-winning director brings
a different level of insight than a general class instructor — and that shows up
in the results.

## Typical formats

- **Private coaching** — one-on-one sessions focused on your goals.
- **Online coaching** — the same personalized work from anywhere.
- **Self-tape / audition feedback** — targeted notes on a specific tape.
- **Workshops** — a lower per-session cost in a small-group setting.

See how these break down on the [coaching options](/#services) page.

## How to judge value (not just price)

The cheapest coach is rarely the best value. Look for real industry credits,
on-camera specialization, and coaching that's tailored to *you* — not a
one-size-fits-all curriculum. One booking can pay for a lot of coaching.
